# Patient Records System for Care Facilities

**Early-stage SaaS Experiment** | In Development

Building a daily records and workflow management system for retirement homes in Mexico. The goal is to simplify how care staff document patient activities, medications, and health observations while preparing for future compliance requirements.

## Status

Active development, exploring product-market fit with initial client feedback.

## Focus Areas

- Data modeling for patient records and care workflows
- Workflow validation and staff coordination
- Building for operational efficiency first
- Compliance planning for later phases

## Technologies

React, Supabase
